SnapLite — Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026

Overview

SnapLite is a browser screenshot extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data SnapLite accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

SnapLite does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking scripts are used.
  • No data is sent to any remote server.
  • No user accounts or sign-ups are required.

Data Storage

Screenshots and settings are stored locally on your device using the Chrome storage.local API. This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly download or copy it.

Permissions

SnapLite requests the following browser permissions:

  • activeTab — To capture the currently active tab when you initiate a screenshot.
  • scripting — To inject content scripts for area selection, DOM element selection, and full-page scrolling capture.
  • storage — To save your settings and screenshot history locally.
  • tabs — To identify the active tab and retrieve page information (URL, title) for screenshot metadata.
  • contextMenus — To add right-click menu options for quick screenshot access.
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) — Required to capture screenshots on any website you visit. SnapLite only activates when you explicitly trigger a capture.

Third-Party Services

SnapLite does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs.

Changes

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions, please open an issue on our project repository or contact the developer directly.

SnapLite — 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 3 月

概述

SnapLite 是一款浏览器截屏扩展。我们致力于保护您的隐私。本政策说明 SnapLite 访问了哪些数据以及如何处理。

数据收集

SnapLite 不收集、传输或共享任何个人数据：

  • 不使用任何分析或追踪脚本。
  • 不向任何远程服务器发送数据。
  • 不需要用户注册或登录。

数据存储

截图和设置仅通过 Chrome storage.local API 存储在您的本地设备上。除非您主动下载或复制，否则数据不会离开浏览器。

权限说明

  • activeTab — 截图时捕获当前活动标签页。
  • scripting — 注入内容脚本，用于区域选择、DOM 元素选择和整页滚动截图。
  • storage — 在本地保存设置和截图历史。
  • tabs — 识别活动标签页并获取页面信息（URL、标题）用于截图元数据。
  • contextMenus — 添加右键菜单选项以快速截图。
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) — 需要在您访问的任何网站上执行截图。SnapLite 仅在您主动触发截图时激活。

第三方服务

SnapLite 不集成任何第三方服务、API 或 SDK。

变更

如本政策更新，将在此页面反映更改并注明新的生效日期。

联系方式

如有任何问题，请在项目仓库中提交 Issue 或直接联系开发者。
